How to Download Canon Printer Driver?

By PrinterSetupGuide • June 2025

Introduction

If you own a Canon printer and need to install it on your computer, downloading the correct driver is essential. Canon drivers ensure your printer functions properly, with full access to all features including scanning, duplex printing, and advanced settings. This guide takes you step-by-step through the process of downloading the right driver from Canon’s official support site.

Go to Canon Support

Step 1: Identify Your Printer Model

Please check the printer’s label or box to find the exact model number—such as Canon PIXMA MG3620 or Canon imageCLASS MF244dw. You’ll need this to locate the correct driver.

Step 2: Visit Canon’s Official Support Website

Click the button above or go to Canon’s official support page. This ensures you’re downloading safe, unmodified drivers directly from Canon.

Step 3: Search for Your Printer Model

  1. Enter your printer model (e.g., PIXMA MG3620) into the search box.
  2. Select your printer from the search results.
  3. Choose your operating system (Windows or macOS).

Step 4: Download the Driver

Under the Drivers & Downloads section, find the recommended driver. Click “Download” and save the installer to your computer.

Step 5: (Optional) Check File Integrity

If a checksum is provided, you can verify the file using built-in tools:

sha256sum Canon_MG3620_Win64_Driver.exe

This helps ensure the file wasn’t corrupted or tampered with.

Step 6: Install the Printer Driver

  • Double-click the downloaded installer.
  • Follow the on-screen setup prompts.
  • Connect your printer when instructed by the installer.
  • Finish the process, then print a test page.

Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Ensure your operating system is up to date.
  • Disconnect and reconnect the printer if installation stalls.
  • Use Canon’s My Printer utility for driver updates.

Conclusion

Downloading the correct Canon printer driver is fast and secure when done via Canon’s official support site. This allows you to unlock all your printer’s features and maintain optimal performance. Ready to set up your Canon printer? Click the button above to get started!
